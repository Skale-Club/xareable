<objective>
Wire all four image-generation flows through `getActiveImageProvider()`. This is the most invasive plan in the phase — touches 4 routes and 2 services — but each wiring is a localized swap (direct Gemini call → provider call). The carousel service refactor is the largest single change (slide 1 = `provider.generate`, slides 2..N = `provider.edit` with slide-1 buffer as `currentImage`).

Purpose: Make the entire image pipeline provider-agnostic so admin's flip in 12-05 actually changes behavior across all flows. Satisfies PROV-07.

Output: Modified routes + modified services; no new files.

Critical constraint: The text-model (Gemini master text plan in carousel, pre-screen in enhancement) is NOT replaced. Only image calls go through the provider (per research Pitfall 4 — keep `apiKey` field for text calls).
</objective>
