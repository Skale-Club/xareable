Outcome derivation (CONTEXT D-04): pass on first verify (attempts=0, verified=true) → "pass"; verified=true after repair (attempts>=1, repaired=true) → "repair_succeeded"; verified=false after exhausting passes → "repair_failed"; repaired=false but verified=false should not occur with current logic, but if it does, treat as "repair_failed". The single emitted log row's attempt_count = attempts. "repair_triggered" is conceptually a synonym for "we tried to repair at least once", and per CONTEXT D-04 we emit ONE row per invocation reflecting the FINAL outcome — do NOT emit per repair pass.
